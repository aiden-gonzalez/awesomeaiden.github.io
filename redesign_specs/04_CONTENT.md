## Tone notes

- **Never use exclamation points.** The voice is dry, observational, slightly literary. Field journal, not corporate blog.
- **Sentence case everywhere.** "Software developer" not "Software Developer." Goes for headings, buttons, labels.
- **Specifics over abstractions.** "double cortado, 9 a.m." not "I like coffee." "80–90% perf boost" not "improved performance."
- **No "I love" or "I'm passionate about."** Show, don't tell. The cycling metaphor itself is the love letter; don't explain it.
- **Cycling vocabulary is OK in moderation.** "Big ring," "tempo," "cadence," "KOM" — these reward attentive readers. Don't translate them; let people who know, know.
